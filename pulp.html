  • Fluff pulp: Germany / Belgium

     Fluff pulp: Germany / Belgium

     Fluff pulp is mainly used for hygiene products such as diapers , napkins , sanitary pads etc.. The main difference to standard market pulp is the density (bulk) of the sheet after drying. In general practice, fluff pulp is produced as rolls on a drying machine. The aim of it is to create a uniform sheet with qualities such as paper density, moisture, and strength. The pulp might also be impregnated with debonders before drying to ease defibration.
