Welcome to Bien Mercantile

We are Bien Mercantile Private Limited, an International Trading House based in Chennai, Tamilnadu, India.

We are having wide range of experience in dealing with Industrial Raw Materials products like Industrial Chemicals, Papers & Boards - Kraft Papers, Art Papers and Boards, Waxes - Fully / Semi Refined Paraffin Waxes, Palm Waxes, Slack Waxes, Stearic Acid, etc., dealing in Machineries for making Cement Block, Hollow Block, Pavement Blocks, Garden Blocks etc., various other New / Used Machinery, all kinds of Metal Scraps in bulk quantities, Waste Paper, Wood Pulp for Paper making and various other products.

We have our associate and Business Partners in Canada, China, Taiwan, Japan, USA, Malaysia, Indonesia, Africa, Russia, Sweden etc., in more than 20 countries across the globe, our ultimate Goal is to find the reputed source and supply the best quality of material at the optimum value for our customers.
